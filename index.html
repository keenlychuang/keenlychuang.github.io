About Me

"He got that dog in him"

Hey y’all! I’m a curious engineer working to translate code and research into technology and action to build a better world for future generations. Outside of work, you can usually find me reading and reviewing books, cafe-hopping around town, or working on my personal fitness.

I currently work on data engineering and implementation of AI/ML systems in the public sector. Previously, I was a research assistant at IBM and the MIT Probabilistic Computing Project, a digital engineer at the Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Research Center, and a student-researcher at MIT CSAIL.

I’m always interested in working at the intersection of technology, intelligence, humanity, and service. I am currently focusing on delving into cloud environments and data modeling at scale. Whoever you are, Wherever you are, whenever you are, I hope you’re happy, sleeping well, and living well!
